PROCEDURE

Basic Knowledge
  1. Read and understand all instructions and questions provided
  2. Answer all questions and click on the "Submit Test" button
  3. Upon successful completion, click on the "Next Level" button which will appear in place of the "Submit" button
Configuration
  1. Select frequency, distance, and fluid type
  2. Click on "CHECK CONFIGURATION" button
  3. Use the formula and calculate time (ms) and click on submit button
    4. Formula: Time = [(Distance * 2) /Speed of Sound] X 1000
  4. You need to take 5 readings (calculations) by selecting different configurations (frequency, distance and fluid type)
  5. After completing 5 readings, click on "GO FOR LEVEL MEASUREMENT" button.
  6. Configuration for level mesurement: Select Tank Height (cm), and water level for level measurement
  7. Click on "CHECK CONFIGURATION" button
Mimic
  1. Fill the Tank: Click the "Inlet" valve to start filling the tank with water
  2. Start Measurement: Click the "START" button to begin the level measurement process
  3. Enter Calculated Time: Input the time you calculated earlier in the provided textbox, then click the "SUBMIT" button
  4. Stop the Measurement: Click the "STOP" button on the mimic frame to stop the measurement
  5. Repeat and Take 4 Readings: Repeat the measurement steps 4 times, each time taking a new reading
  6. Finish the Process: After completing 4 readings, click the "RESULT" button to conclude the level measurement process
